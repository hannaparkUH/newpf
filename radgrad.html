RadGrad UI Redesign

"Developing awesome STEM professionals, one graduate at a time"

BACKGROUND

RadGrad is a website created for Computer Science students at University of Hawaii at Manoa to improve the undergraduate STEM degree experience.students. During the summer of 2020 and the spring of 2021, I had an opportunity to be a part of the RadGrad team lead by Professor Philip Johnson. We upgraded the original RadGrad using Typescript, React, and other technologies not present in the original RadGrad.

My role was to redesign the website and develop HTML/CSS. I modified the layouts, added more graphic elements and improved navigation for better conversions and site performance.
